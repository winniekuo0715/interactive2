Oops! typeface

      My typeface is built up with two parts. One is filled bold shape and the other is thin storke. It displays the contrast of the letter in different way. The thin storkes do not show the whole thing in the part because I want to show the effect that even you take away some part of filled place in the letter, you still can tell which the letter is.
      The trick I used on the alphabet is that when you passing on each letter, it will explose really quick and go back.
      It is inspired by my own experience of something holding in my hand sometimes almost falling down when I didn't pay attention on it. That's the reason why I named it oops!
